I teach political theory at Hamilton College. I am a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Government Department. I work also with the Arthur Levitt Public Affairs Center

My Vita

Most of my teaching and research has been about ideas about work and democracy in American Political Thought. My current work focuses on the varieties of work ethics, the history of social science, and the link between those two topics, and the development of democratic thought. I'm also interested in the settlement movement, and the public practice of the liberal arts.
